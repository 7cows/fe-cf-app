Privacy Policy for the Mortgage Calculation API

Effective Date: 24.06.2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to our Mortgage Calculation API. We are committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices concerning the collection, use, and disclosure of your data when you use our API. By accessing and using our API, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information Collection

We collect and process the following data:

  • Loan Amount: The principal amount of the mortgage you wish to calculate.
  • Interest Rate: The rate at which interest will be calculated on your loan.
  • Loan Term: The duration for which the loan will be extended.
  • Monthly Payment: The expected monthly payment amount.
  • Summary Data: Short deanonymized summaries that serve for the input parameters (as applicable).

3. Use of Data

The data collected is used solely for the purpose of calculating mortgage-related figures and providing related summaries. These calculations help in providing accurate and tailored financial information based on your inputs.

4. Data Storage and Security

We store your data only for the duration necessary to perform calculations and immediately dispose of data post-processing. We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information when you enter, submit, or access your personal information.

5. Disclosure of Data

We do not share your data with third parties except as necessary to provide our services or as required by law.

6. Your Rights

As our API does not store any personal data, there are no facilities for accessing or deleting personal data. We value your privacy and ensure that no personal information is retained after your interaction with our services.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
